PERSONALITIES

HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)

Date Of Birth: 12 Rabiulawal, 570CE
Date Of Demise: Passed from this world in 632 CE
Birth Place: Makkah
Place Of Farewell: Madinah
Age: 63 Years
Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) is the last prophet of Allah and
had been sent a blessing and source of guidence for the whole
universe. The first ayat of Quran was revealed upon him at the
age of 40 in the cave of Hira. He spent 13 years in Makkah after
first revelatioin and then migrated to Madinah, where he spent 10
years preaching Islam to people through his ahadees and sunnah which
were explaination of Holy Quran.
Narrated By Ibn' Abbas:
"The Prophet (PBUH) embraced me and said, 'O Allah! Teach him the
Book (Quran)'".

HAZRAT ABU BAKR (R.A.)

Date Of Birth: October 27, 573 AD
Date Of Demise: August 23, 634 AD
Birth Place: Makkah
Place Of Farewell: Madinah
Age: 60 Years
Hazrat Abu Bakr was the first caliph to Muslim ummah after Holy
Prophet (PBUH) passed on from this world. His full name is Abdullah ibn
Abu Quhafah Usman bin Aamer Al Quareshi Al Taymi. Hazrat Abu Bakr was the man (Adult) to accept Islam. He was given the title of Siddiq (the one who accepted the truth) at the event of Miraj when no one believed at what Holy Prophet (PBUH) told, he was the only person to say that He (PBUH) is telling the truth. "Abdullah reported Allah's Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:
'If I were to choose as my bosom friend I would have chosen the son of Abu Quhafa (Abu Bakr) as my bosom friend.'"


HAZRAT UMAR (R.A.)

Date Of Birth: Born in 585 AD
Date Of Demise: November 3, 644 AD
Birth Place: Makkah
Place Of Farewell: Madinah
Age: 59 Years
Hazrat Umar was the second caliph to Muslim Ummah after Hazrat Abu Bakr
passed away, he accepted islam at the age of 39. After accepting Islam
, it was for the first time that Muslims in the imamat of Holy Prophet (PBUH)
offered prayer openly in Masjid e haram. It was in his time that following
systems were introduced, Department of investigation of complaints
against officers of state, army as state department, The concept of welfare.
"Ibn Umar reported: The Messenger of Allah (PBUH) said, 'O Allah, strengthen
Islam with one of two men whom you love more: Abu Jahl or

HAZRAT USMAN (R.A.)

Date Of Birth: 576 or 579 AD
Date Of Demise: June 17, 656 AD
Birth Place: Taif
Place Of Farewell: Madinah
Age: 80 Years
Hazrat Usman was the third caliph to Muslim ummah after Hazrat
Umar. He centralized the Islamic government and established an
official version of Quran. His death marked the beginning of open religious
conflicts and political conflicts within the Islamic community. he was
given the titles of Zhu al-Nurayn (As he married two daughters of Holy
Propeht (PBUH)) and ghanni (As he spent all of his wealth in the path of Allah.
Holy Prophet (PBUH) said: "Every Prophet will have a special companion in heaven and my compainion will be Usman".


HAZRAT ALI (R.A.)

Date Of Birth: March 17, 599 AD
Date Of Demise: January 29, 661 AD
Birth Place: Makkah
Place Of Farewell: Kufa
Age: 61 Years
Hazrat Ali was the forth caliph of the islamic ummah after hazrat usman.
Hazrat ali was the first child to accept islam. he was the cousin of Holy
Prophet (PBUH) as well as later married Hazrat Fatima (daughter of Holy
Prophet (PBUH)) and became his (PBUH) son-in-law. He was a brave man
and a powerful warrior. He participated in every battle fought during the
life of Holy Prophet (PBUH) and brought victory to muslims.
The Holy Prophet (PBUH) said :
"There is no sword but Dhul-Fiqar, and there is no man but Ali (R.A)"
