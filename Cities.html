CITIES

ABBOTTABAD

Abbottabad, the capital city of Abbottabad district is located in Hanza
region of eastern Khyber pakhtunkhwa. It is the 40th largest city in
Pakistan and forth largest in the province of Khyber pakhtunkhwa by
population. it is about 120 km (75 mi) North of Islamabad-Rawalpindi and
150 km (95 mi) east of Peshawar, at an elavation of 1,256 m (4,121 ft).
Kashmir lies a short distance to the east. Abbottabad was founded and
named after Major james Abbott in january 1853.

NARAN

Naran, is a town in upper Kaghan valley in Mansehra district of
Khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan. It is located 119 Km (74 mi)
from Mansehra city at the altitude of 2,409 m (7,904 ft). It is located
about 65 km (40 mi) from Babusar Top. it is one of the most popular
tourist attraction locally as well as internationally. naran Kaghan is
famous as a tourist spot owing to it's pleasent weather in peak season
every year thousands rush to explore the valley.


MAKKAH

Makkah, Arabic city is located in western Saudi Arabia. in the sirat
mountains, inland from the red sea coast. It is the holiest of Muslim cities.
Muhammad, the last Prophet of Islam, was born in Makkah and it is
towards this religious centre that Muslims turn five times daily (Qiblah).
All able Muslims attmept a hajj (pilgrimage) to Makkah at least once in
their lifetime. because it is sacred, only Muslims are allowed to enter the
city.

MADINAH

Madinah, formally, Al-Madinah al-Munawwarah (the luminous city) or
Madinah Rasul Allah (City of the Prophet (PBUH)), anciently known as
Yasrib, city located in the hijaz region of western Saudi Arabia, about
100 miles inland from the red sea and 275 miles from Makkah by road.
It is second holiest city in islam after Makkah. madinah is celebrated as
the place from which Muhammad (PBUH) established the Muslim
community (Ummah).
